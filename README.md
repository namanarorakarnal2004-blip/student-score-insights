## 🔍 Project Overview
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a Techno Sales dataset covering *5,095 orders* across *35 Indian states*. The goal is to uncover sales trends, brand performance, profit margins, and regional insights.

---

## 📁 Dataset
| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| Records | 5,095 orders |
| States | 35 |
| Categories | 14 (SSD, CPU, RAM, GPU, etc.) |
| Brands | 11 (Samsung, Intel, Nvidia, etc.) |
| Columns | 14 |

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- *Python* — Pandas, NumPy
- *Visualization* — Matplotlib, Seaborn
- *Environment* — Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📈 Analysis Performed
- ✅ Data Cleaning & Null Value Treatment
- ✅ Descriptive Statistics
- ✅ Sales by Category & Brand
- ✅ Order Status Distribution
- ✅ Top 10 States by Revenue
- ✅ Profit Margin Analysis
- ✅ Correlation Heatmap
- ✅ Top Supervisors by Revenue

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Revenue heavily concentrated in a few key states
- Strong correlation between Quantity and Total Sales
- Delivered orders form the majority of all transactions
- Significant profit variation across product categories
